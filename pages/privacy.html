Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

Voice Diary ("we", "our", "the app") is a personal journaling app that lets you write or speak your diary entries. This page explains what data the app uses, how it's stored, and what choices you have.

The short version: your diary entries — text and voice — are stored locally on your own device. We do not run our own servers that collect or store your diary content.

1. What data we use

  • Diary entries: text and audio recordings you create are saved using your browser's local storage on your device.
  • Microphone access: requested only when you tap the record button, and only to capture the voice note you choose to create. Audio is not sent anywhere unless you explicitly export or share it.
  • App settings: preferences like theme, accent color, and app-lock PIN are stored locally to remember your setup between sessions.

2. What we don't do

  • We don't upload your diary entries or recordings to a server without your action (e.g. an explicit export/share feature).
  • We don't sell or share your personal diary content with third parties.
  • We don't track what you write or say.

3. Local storage & data loss

Because your entries live in your browser/device storage, clearing your browser data, uninstalling the app, or switching devices without a backup can permanently delete your diary. We recommend using any built-in export feature periodically if you want a backup copy.

4. Third-party services

The app may load fonts and small libraries (for example, PDF export) from third-party content delivery networks (CDNs) such as Google Fonts and cdnjs. These services may log basic technical information like IP address as part of standard web delivery — this is governed by their own privacy policies, not ours.

5. Advertising

If this site displays advertisements (for example, through Google AdSense), those ad providers may use cookies or similar technologies to serve ads based on your visits to this and other sites. You can learn more about how Google uses data in this context at Google's Partner Sites policy. You can opt out of personalized advertising through Google Ads Settings.

6. Children's privacy

Voice Diary is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
